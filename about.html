Welcome To LEOFAT

We are your one-stop shop for all printing services, event management, and local drinks. Established in 2010, we are located in the Doryumu Habitat off Tema Akosombo Road. Our commitment is to provide high-quality services to meet all your needs. Contact us at 0245278708 or 0546956977 for more information.
